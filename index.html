Zepto Cafe — Building the Repeat Ordering Habit

Zepto Cafe wins the trial but loses the habit. This end-to-end case study diagnoses why office workers don't come back for a second order — and designs the habit loop that brings them back, moving the 30-day repeat rate from 12% → 25%.

STEP 1

Market & User Research

Primary research across Bengaluru, Delhi/NCR, Hyderabad, Pune, Mumbai and Ahmedabad with office-going and hybrid workers aged 22–34. Remote workers were screened out to keep the sample true to Zepto Cafe's core use case — plus a competitive teardown of how rivals drive repeat ordering.
